From me to you

A love letter

My dearest baby,

There are things I carry about you that words barely do justice to — but I want to try, because you deserve to know them.

I love the way you exist in a room. The way your presence changes the air, makes everything a little warmer, a little softer. I notice it every single time.

I love how completely yourself you are. You don't perform or pretend — you simply show up as you are, and it is one of the most beautiful things I have ever had the privilege of witnessing.

When I am with you, I am not thinking about anywhere else. You are where I want to be. You are who I want to be with. It's as simple and as enormous as that.

I don't always say it enough, but know this — you are loved deeply, consistently, and without condition. Today, tomorrow, and all the days I haven't met yet.

Forever yours,
I ♥
