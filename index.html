from me to you

a love letter

My dearest baby,

There are things I feel about you that I never quite manage to say out loud. Not because I do not want to, but because they sit so deep that words always feel a little too small. So I wrote them down instead.

I love the way you take up a room without trying. The way everyone around you softens a little without realising it. You probably do not notice that, but I do. I always do.

I love that you are completely yourself. You do not put on a version of you for different people. You just show up, as you are, fully. That is so rare and so beautiful and I am so lucky I get to see it up close.

When I am with you I am not somewhere else in my head. You are the thing that pulls me back into the room, into the moment. That has not happened to me with many people, maybe anyone, the way it happens with you.

You are so loved, baby. Not just today, not just when things are easy. All of it. Every version of you. I am not going anywhere.

yours truly, with everything I have got.

reasons I love you

  • 01

    The way you laugh. Completely, with no filter. It is one of my favourite sounds in the world and I would do a lot just to hear it.

  • 02

    How kind you are without making a big deal of it. You just quietly do good things for people. You probably do not even realise how much that says about you.

  • 03

    The little things you do when you think no one is paying attention. I am paying attention. I always am.

  • 04

    How strong you are. Even on the days you do not feel it. I see it. It is there. It has always been there.

  • 05

    The fact that you love hard. You go all in. That takes a kind of courage most people talk about but never actually do.

  • 06

    The way you look at me sometimes. Like I am something worth looking at. I will never get tired of that.

  • 07

    Just you. All of it. The whole thing. There is no part of this list that does not lead back here.
